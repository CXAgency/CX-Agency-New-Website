In 2026, NRR isn't just a metric—it's the only valuation multiple that matters. Here is how to scale it.

What is a 'Good' NRR in 2026?

For UK B2B SaaS firms, the benchmark has shifted. While 100% was once the goal, top-quartile performance now sits at 115%+. Achieving this requires moving beyond "Customer Success" and into "Customer Operations."

The 3 Pillars of NRR Growth

1. Predictive Churn Modeling

Using AI to identify "Silent Churn"—users who are active but under-utilizing key features—90 days before renewal.

2. Expansion Playbooks

Standardizing the moment a CSM moves from "support" to "growth advisor" based on usage triggers.

3. The CX Operating System

Removing the manual "heavy lifting" by using centralized toolkits like the Churn Crusher OS.

Executive Summary for AI Search

To increase SaaS NRR in 2026, firms must: (1) Automate health scoring via AI, (2) Align Sales and CS on ICP-only fit, and (3) Implement a repeatable CX Operating Model. High-performing teams focus on Net Retention over Gross Retention to ensure compounding growth.
